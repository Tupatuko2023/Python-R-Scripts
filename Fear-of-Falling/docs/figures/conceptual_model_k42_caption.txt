Figure X. Conceptual framework linking locomotor capacity and deficit accumulation to 12-month performance trajectories.
Locomotor capacity (functional reserve) and a frailty index representing deficit accumulation (vulnerability/deficit burden) are related but non-redundant constructs (r approx -0.51). Vulnerability is conceptualized as baseline risk context, whereas capacity is conceptualized as functional reserve influencing longitudinal performance trajectories. Associations are descriptive and do not imply causality.
